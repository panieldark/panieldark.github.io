About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel! I am a curious, retentive, and often self-effacing extrovert pursuing a computer science degree at the United States Military Academy. I am actively seeking out a software engineering internship / co-op position.

If you are considering bringing me onto your team, there are some quick things you should know: Above all, I can guarantee that I will be 110% committed to learning and helping out in any way possible. I am open to the prospect of wearing many hats and late nights. I am not scared to find out how that I have lots still to learn; I cherish this.

I have spent two years at West Point, a place globally known to be a challenging and developmental experience. Ultimately, it allows me to guarantee: I am not a person that rolls into the office by 1130. I will be there bright and early, committed to develop myself and the project at hand. Once I commit myself to something, I care about it. I become obsessed with developing and improving it. And once developed, I continue maintaining it and think about how it can become better.

I hope to offer my perspective and passion for solving complex problems to a like-minded team this summer. Check out my resume below to find out more about me and what I've done.
